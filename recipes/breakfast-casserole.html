Breakfast Casserole

Breakfast for one.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces of ground pork or turkey sausage
  • 1 Large egg
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1/8 tsp of salt and pepper
  • 2 cups cubed french, italian, or sourdough bread
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar or monterey jack cheese

Directions

  1. Cook sausage until no longer pink. Transfer to plate and set aside.
  2. In a medium sized bowl, beat egg. Whisk in milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place bread in a small buttered baking dish. Sprinkle bread with the cooked sausage and cheese. Pour egg mixture over top.
  4. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  5. Let casserole warm to room temperature. Preheat oven to 350 F. Bake casserole for 25 to 30 minutes or until egg mixture is set.
