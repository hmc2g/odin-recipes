Single Serve Mac and Cheese

A simple dish for one.

Ingredients

  • 1 Serving of pasta of your choosing
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 2 Tbsp Flour
  • 2/3-3/4 cup Milk
  • 1/4 tsp Mustard Powder
  • 1/2 cup Shredded Cheese of your own choosing

Directions

  1. Bring salted water to a boil in a medium sauce pan. Prepare pasta according to the directons on the package. Drain.
  2. In the same sauce pan, on medium heat, melt butter until it is simmering. Sprinkle flour over the melted butter and whisk to form a pasta. Allow the pasta to cook until golden brown. Slowly whisk in 2/3 cup of milk until it creates a smooth sauce. If a thinner sauce is desired use 3/4 cup. Sprinkle in mustard powder. Add in shredded cheese and stir until thick and creamy.
  3. Add in drained pasta and stir into the cheese sauce until pasta is covered. Serve immediately.
